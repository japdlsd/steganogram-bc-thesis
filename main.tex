\chapter{Goal of this work}

In this chapter we will specify theoretical and techincal 
requirements on our application.

\begin{itemize}
    \item main functionality: embed and extract compressed encrypted 
          messages into JPEG images with stegokey and cryptokey
    \item strong cryptography
    \item strong steganography
    \item compression algorithm optimized for text
    \item support of UTF-8 text
    \item design requirement: possiblitiy of change of used libraries
    \item prediction of maximum message length based on stegongraphy algorithm and cover image
    \item possibility of splitting long (potentially unsecure) message into smaller ones and embedding
          into several messages
    \item possibility of sending message via social media and/or messengers
    \item ability to withstand additional compression of images of different communication channels
\end{itemize}

\chapter{Theoretical aspects of application}

In this chapter we will talk about steganographic communication protocol,
discuss various possible communication channels (carriers), describe
used algorithms (both for steganography and cryptography) with concrete
settings. Then we will talk about limits of algorithms we've chosen and
how to maximize their strength by follow "good policies" of usage.
